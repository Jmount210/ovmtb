The OVMTB team is made up of student-athletes, coaches, parents, and faculty. We are an umbrella organization that provides coaching, support, practices, and development activities for high school teams that race in the Arizona High School Cycling League.

We're currently supporting teams from all of the high schools listed below along with several from their feeder middle schools.

  • Ironwood Ridge High School
  • Canyon del Oro High School
  • Pusch Ridge Christian Academy
  • Mountain View High School
  • Flowing Wells High School
  • Salpointe Catholic High School
  • Leman Academy of Excellence
  • Homeschool
