We are an inclusive club, focusing on the development of young men and women via the sport of mountain bike racing. We use mountain biking to bring kids together, enabling them to grow confidence, environmental awareness, fitness, and friendship. Welcome to our site.

We are an independent 501(c)(3) non-profit public charity, and we participate as part of the Arizona Interscholastic Cycling League, an official affiliate of the  National Interscholastic Cycling Association.

Check out our Facebook page for pictures and results from our Fall 2018 season. Preseason activities for the Fall 2019 season start up in May and June, and full practices start July 1st. See the event calendar or contact us for more info.

Interested? Don't hesitate to reach out. The club is open to all high school and middle school riders in the Oro Valley  and Marana area.

We are always taking on new student-athletes, from middle school up. Contact us with any questions.

We are always accepting tax-deductible donations and discussions regarding team sponsorship and support.
